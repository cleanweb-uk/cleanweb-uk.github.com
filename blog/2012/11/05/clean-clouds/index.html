Upcoming: Clean Clouds

Posted by James Smith
Nov 5th, 2012

It’s soon time for Cleanweb London’s monthly meeting, and this month we’re trying something a little different. After the success of our Ignite Cleanweb event in September, we’ve decided to try putting on more talks.

So, we’re excited to announce our first talk night on 19th November, 7pm at the Open Data Institute. We’ve got three great speakers lined up who are going to talk about cloud computing and its environmental impact:

  • Francine Bennett, Mastodon C: Reducing the environmental impact of big data.
  • Nigel Stevens, Infinity SDC: Creating environmentally-friendly datacentres.
  • Andy Piper, VMware: Cloud Foundry, the open source cloud computing platform

Talks will be finished by 9, so we’ll be heading to a nearby pub for a drink and a chat afterwards.

Places are limited, so sign up now to avoid disappointment!
