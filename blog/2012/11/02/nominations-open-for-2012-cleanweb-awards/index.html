Nominations Now Open for 2012 Cleanweb Awards

Posted by James Smith
Nov 2nd, 2012

On 17th December, we’ll be holding our first Cleanweb Awards ceremony at Forward Technology in Camden. It’ll be a fun evening celebrating all the achievments of the cleanweb community, and giving out a few awards as well.

As it’s community-driven, we want your nominations for the awards! Just fill in the form below to let us know who you think should be in the running.

There are six different categories:

Overall Impact
Which project, individual or business has made the biggest impact through online sustainability this year?
Business
Which is the most exciting cleanweb business in the UK right now?
Open Source
What’s the best open source project working on cleanweb ideas?
Open Data
Who’s making the world more sustainable with open data?
Communications
Data journalism, science communications, PR - who’s getting the message out to a wider audience?
Dirtyweb
Wilfully ignoring the future? Pretending to be clean while not really bothering? Which organisation do you want to name and shame?

Nominations close at midnight on November 23rd 2012.
