Ignite Cleanweb Talk Details

Posted by James Smith
Sep 5th, 2012

Just 8 days to go until Ignite Cleanweb comes to London!

We’ve nearly finalised the list of talks, and have put the titles and speakers up on our Ignite page.

So, come along to Forward Technology on Thursday 13th September, and learn how Loco2 took on the European Rail Booking Monster, or maybe how to live Offgrid in the City. How about Good Food, For Everyone, Forever, courtesy of Sustaination, or how open hardware can help save energy with OpenEnergyMonitor?

Places are limited, so register now to make sure you don’t miss out! If you can’t make it, we’ll be streaming live video and posting the talks online afterwards, so you don’t have to miss out.

Also, if you’re an organisation and would like to buy the beers in the pub afterwards, please get in touch!
